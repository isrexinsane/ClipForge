# Epic 11 — App Store Submission

**Status:** ⬜ Not Started

## Scope

Prepare and submit ClipForge to Apple App Store.

**App Store Listing:**
- App name: ClipForge
- Subtitle: "Create reaction GIFs from social media moments"
- Category: Utilities
- Screenshots: Link paste → trim → GIF export workflow
- Preview video: 30-second walkthrough of GIF creation

**Required Legal Elements:**
- Privacy policy (link in listing)
- Copyright notice in app Settings/About
- DMCA compliance documentation

**Submission Strategy:**
- Exclude YouTube to reduce rejection risk (can add post-launch)
- Frame as GIF creation tool, not video downloader
- Include demo video showing creative intent
- App Review note explaining the GIF workflow
